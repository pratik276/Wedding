Our Story

On February 13, 2021, the air was crisp and the night felt alive. He was out with his friends, celebrating a special moment at a hilltop farmhouse. The laughter echoed across the open skies, the music blending with the crackling of the bonfire, but somewhere deep within, there was a void. As much as he enjoyed the company, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was missing in his life. He yearned for someone special, someone whose presence could fill the silence he often felt after the noise faded. That night, as he gazed out into the vast horizon, he quietly wished for someone who would sing to his heart.

Miles away, she lay at home, her dreams swirling in the stillness of the night. She had always been a dreamer, picturing a world where everything fell into place as if destiny had written it. That night, her dreams were vivid, but she had no idea that her life was about to take a turn she had only imagined.

The next day, February 14, 2021, the day of love, he returned home, the warmth of the farmhouse fading into memories. With Valentine's Day all around, the desire for companionship stirred within him stronger than ever. He wished he could find a girl—someone who would not only understand him but sing for him, filling his life with melody.

As fate would have it, that very day, she was returning to her hostel. Her father was driving her, and though her mind was wandering, something magical was about to happen. A chat started between them, a simple exchange that felt anything but ordinary. It was as if the gods had finally answered his wish. She, sweet and gentle, took the time to chat with him, despite being on the road with her dad. What began as casual conversation quickly turned into something more. It was as though they had been waiting for each other without even knowing it.

Both of them, though content in their lives, had been yearning for something deeper, something to bring them joy. And in each other, they found exactly that. They began to look forward to the day they would meet. Though the days passed slowly, there was always a sense of excitement in the air, a shared hope that they would soon be face to face.

And then, that moment arrived. When they finally met, it felt like the missing pieces of their lives had come together. There was no looking back from that day onward. In their ups and downs, they stood by each other, becoming pillars of strength and support. Whether it was the small struggles of daily life or the daunting task of convincing their parents to agree to their marriage, they faced it all, hand in hand.

Their love was tested, but never broken. And with all the twists and turns, they finally reached the day that had seemed so distant. On February 13, 2025, exactly four years from the day he had stood on that hilltop farmhouse and wished for someone like her, they will finally get married. It was as though the universe had conspired to bring them together, and now, as they prepare to step into the next chapter of their lives, they do so knowing that they were always meant to be.

From a simple chat on a Valentine’s Day to a love that withstood every challenge, their story is one of serendipity, struggle, and joy. A story that began with a wish under the stars, and now, with the same stars as their witnesses, they will say their vows, sealing a love that was written in the stars all along.

On February 13, 2025—four years to the day he made that silent wish—Ashma will be getting married to Pratik.

This is our story: one of serendipity, love, and a future that’s just beginning.
